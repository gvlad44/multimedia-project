About us

We are a non-profit organization established in 2020 which cares deeply for the well-being of animals. Our main concern is to find a loving home to the stray dogs we rescued that are eager to bring happiness into your lives. Your new friends live in a compound composed of two buildings, one in which they live (Dogs quarters) and one where they pay a visit when they feel unwell (Medical quarters)
